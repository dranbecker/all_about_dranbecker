Dr. med. Andreas Becker is Head of the Department of Neurology and Medical Director at SRH Kurpfalzkrankenhaus Heidelberg.
He is a board-certified neurologist with an additional qualification in intensive care medicine.
He completed his medical studies at the University of Hamburg.
He subsequently worked in academic and clinical positions at the University of Marburg, including on the neurological intensive care unit as well as in Parkinson’s disease, stroke, and memory care.
In addition, he was involved in health services research and multicenter projects within the Parkinson Competence Network.
After obtaining board certification, he assumed a senior physician position at the neurosurgical intensive care unit of Marburg University Hospital.
Since 2014, Dr. Becker has led the Department of Neurology at SRH Kurpfalzkrankenhaus Heidelberg, and since 2018 he has also served as Medical Director of the hospital.
His clinical and academic focus includes Parkinson’s complex therapy, including device-aided treatments, neurological intensive care medicine with a particular focus on weaning processes, and digital medicine.
Furthermore, Dr. Becker is engaged in the education and training of Parkinson’s nurses and in the development of national structures for Parkinson’s care.
He is an active member of the German Society of Neurology, the German Parkinson’s Society, and Parkinson Networks Germany e.V.
