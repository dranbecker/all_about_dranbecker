Dr. med. Andreas Becker is Head of the Department of Neurology and Medical Director at SRH Kurpfalz Hospital Heidelberg. He is a board-certified specialist in Neurology and Intensive Care Medicine. He studied medicine at the University of Hamburg. He subsequently worked as a research associate at the University of Marburg in the Movement Disorders Working Group. There, he also cared for patients with cognitive impairment in the memory clinic. In addition, he was involved in the development of the Parkinson Competence Network, contributing to health services research and multicenter projects. After obtaining his specialist certification, he took on a senior physician position in the neurosurgical intensive care unit at Marburg University Hospital. Since 2014, Dr. Becker has been Head of the Department of Neurology at SRH Kurpfalz Hospital Heidelberg. Since 2018, he has also served as the hospital’s Medical Director. His clinical areas of expertise include Parkinson’s care, including all device-aided therapies, neurological intensive care medicine with a particular focus on weaning processes, and digital medicine. In addition, Dr. Becker is actively involved in the education and training of Parkinson Nurses and in shaping national structures for Parkinson’s care. He is an active member of the German Society of Neurology, the German Parkinson Society, and Parkinson Networks Germany (Parkinsonnetzwerke Deutschland e.V.).
